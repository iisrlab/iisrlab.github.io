## Education & Language Learning

## AI Mandarin Partner
A teacher-first AI co-pilot for Mandarin lesson design, aligned with Taiwan’s **TBCL** standards and classroom workflows (built on TAIDE / EDU-TAIDE).

**What it enables**
- TBCL level-controlled teaching passages (Levels 1–5)
- Teacher-editable vocabulary lists
- Grammar examples and substitution drills for classroom use
- TOCFL-style practice items (in development)
- Feedback-driven iteration via teacher workshops and pilot use

**Awards / Recognition**
- Supported under a Ministry of Education program to advance the Mandarin education industry (2025).

## Write AI
An AI-assisted essay review system designed to help teachers provide faster, more consistent feedback at classroom scale.

**What it enables**
- Rapid structure suggestions and semantic analysis to support teacher review workflows
- A workflow intended to reduce grading burden and improve feedback timeliness

**Awards / Recognition**
- Officially announced by Taiwan’s Ministry of Education (MOE) as a national-level education innovation initiative (2025).

## ATAIGI（臺語學習 App）
A multimodal, AI-powered learning app for **Taiwanese Hokkien**—co-developed by teams in **Taiwan and Canada**—designed to support low-resource language education with text, images, and speech.

**What it enables**
- Multimodal learning experience (definitions, example sentences, illustrative images, romanized pronunciation, and audio)
- A system built on multiple AI components (including transliteration) for low-resource language learning

**Awards / Recognition**
- **2nd Place**, CTCI (中技社) AI Creative Competition 2025 — *ATAIGI: Multimodal Generative AI Taiwanese Hokkien Learning System*
- **NAACL 2025 Demo Paper** — System Demonstration Track

**Links**
- NAACL 2025 Demo (ACL Anthology): https://aclanthology.org/2025.naacl-demo.2/

## Humanities Knowledge Discovery

## TaihuCais（臺鵠開示）
A conversational knowledge discovery system for Taiwan humanities, designed for dialogue-based exploration over humanities resources with evidence-grounded retrieval.

**What it enables**
- Natural-language querying for humanities knowledge discovery
- Retrieval + evidence-oriented design to improve groundedness and traceability
- A platform for humanities education, research exploration, and cultural institutions

**Awards / Recognition**
- **Future Tech Award (未來科技獎)** — Taiwan Innotech Expo 2025

- Official site: https://www.taihu-ai.com/
- NTU project page: https://taihu.ntu.edu.tw/
