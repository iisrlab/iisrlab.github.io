## Team

### Staff

- **Herena Wang**  
  *Position*: Lab Manager, responsible for the **Edu TAIDE** project  
  Herena plays a crucial role in managing the laboratory and overseeing the implementation of the **Edu TAIDE** project, which focuses on educational AI tools for teaching.

- **Jessie Chang**  
  *Position*: Program Manager, responsible for the **AI CUP** project  
  Jessie oversees the **AI CUP** initiative, Taiwan's largest AI competition, working with various academic institutions to foster AI talent.

- **Joy Han**  
  *Position*: Biomedical Research Lead  
  Joy leads the lab's biomedical NLP initiatives, applying AI techniques to process and analyze biomedical data, and contributing to research in healthcare AI.

### PhD Students

- **Fan-Pin Tseng**  
  *Research Topic*: **Multimodal LLMs in Medicine**  
  Fan-Pin focuses on applying Large Language Models to the medical domain. His recent work (IW-DMRN 2024) integrates Longformer and CRF models to enhance patient privacy protection and time information normalization in electronic health records, specifically addressing challenges in processing long clinical texts.

- **Kevin Chun-Kai Wu** [üÜî ORCID](https://orcid.org/0000-0003-4074-9094)  
  *Research Topic*: **End-to-end LLM Pretraining**  
  Kevin is researching methods for end-to-end pretraining of large language models (LLMs) without the need for pre-tokenization, aiming to improve LLM training efficiency and reduce processing time.
